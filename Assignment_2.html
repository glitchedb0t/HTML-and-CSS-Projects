Best Selling Game Consoles in History

Playstation 2

Stats:

  • Released on 10/26/2000 in the US.
  • The units sold reached 158.70 million worldwide as of 2013.
  • Initially the console cost $299.
  • Best selling game was Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas.

Nintendo DS

Stats:

  • Released on 11/21/2004 in the US.
  • The units sold reached 154.02 million worldwide as of 2016.
  • Initially the console cost $149.
  • Best selling game was New Super Mario Bros.

Nintendo Switch

Stats:

  • Released on 3/3/2017 in the US.
  • The units sold has reached 122.55 million worldwide as of 2022.
  • Initially the console cost $299.
  • Best selling game to this point is Mario Kart 8 Deluxe.
